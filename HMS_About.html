Rodney Hawksford

Master Varnisher / Painter

Rodney Hawksford created Hawksford Marine Service in the 1980's. When creating HMS, his mission was to keep people happy with the way their vessels look and perform. Coming from a line of artisans he naturally began wood restoration and top-side painting. Today Rodney enjoys traveling, varnishing, and gardening. If you are seeking services for your vessel and are lucky you can get him to flex his masterful painting methods using AwlGrip paint for both Hull-side and Top-side paintjobs.

Cherry Esmond

Master Varnisher / Artist

Cherry Esmond along with Rodney created Hawksford Marine Service in the 1980's. While creating HMS Cherry went to college at Ringling College of Art and Design in Sarasota, Florida and got a Bachelor's degree in Fine Arts and Illustration. Cherry enjoys traveling, hiking, painting, varnishing, and loves animals.

Teak Hawksford

Master Varnisher / Natural and Health Scientist

Teak Hawksford is from Sarasota, Florida. He is a member of the Hawksford Marine Service team and strives to provide all his clients with the best varnish skills in the industry.
